# Legal Policy Templates Overview

This page describes the legal policy template files included in the IcedMangoes project and how they fit into the documentation structure.

---

## What Are These Templates?

The IcedMangoes project includes three markdown template files that serve as starting points for common legal policies required by many web applications:

| Template | Purpose | Link |
|----------|---------|------|
| **Privacy Policy** | Explains how user data is collected, used, and protected | [Privacy Policy Template](legal-templates/privacy-policy-template.md) |
| **Terms of Service** | Defines the contractual relationship between the business and users | [Terms Template](legal-templates/terms-template.md) |
| **Cookie Policy** | Describes what cookies are used and why | [Cookie Policy Template](legal-templates/cookie-policy-template.md) |

---

## Important Notice

> **These are templates only. You must customize them for your own business and legal jurisdiction.**

These files are provided as reference material and placeholders. They are **not** legal advice. Before publishing any legal policy:

- Consult a qualified attorney in your jurisdiction
- Adapt the language to your specific business practices
- Ensure compliance with applicable laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA, local data protection regulations)
- Update sections that reference IcedMangoes or placeholder content

---

## How This Fits Into the Project

- **Main README**: [../README.md](../README.md) – Project overview, setup, and disclaimer regarding compliance with data protection and payment processing regulations
- **Documentation Wiki**: [README.md](README.md) – Central documentation hub for setup, guides, and technical reference

The legal templates sit alongside the technical documentation as optional resources for operators who deploy IcedMangoes and need to publish privacy, terms, and cookie policies. They are not part of the application code and do not affect runtime behavior.
