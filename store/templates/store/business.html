{# Protect Your Art — informational only, no processing in-app #}

Protect Your Art (Recommended)

Preprocess images before uploading to reduce AI style mimicry and unauthorized training risk. Use third-party tools on your own device — this app does not process images.

  • 1. Export web-sized images (e.g., 1600–2400px longest edge)
  • 2. Apply Glaze or WebGlaze to protect style
  • 3. Optionally apply Nightshade
  • 4. Upload protected images to your store
  • 5. Keep originals private for printing
