Protect Your Art (Glaze / WebGlaze / Nightshade)

This app does not process images. Use these tools on your own device before uploading.

What These Tools Are

  • Glaze / WebGlaze — Adds subtle alterations to reduce AI style mimicry.
  • Nightshade — Aims to discourage unauthorized training on scraped images.

These are third-party tools from University of Chicago SAND Lab. Review their official docs and terms.

Recommended Workflow

  1. Keep originals in a private master folder
  2. Create web-sized exports (1600–2400px longest edge)
  3. Apply Glaze or WebGlaze to exports
  4. Optionally apply Nightshade
  5. Upload protected images to your store
  6. Never replace originals; use them for prints only
